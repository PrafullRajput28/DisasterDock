Why Practice Fire Drills?

Fires spread quickly and can cause panic. A fire drill helps everyone practice what to do during an emergency so that lives are saved, and injuries are avoided.

This drill will teach you how to react calmly, follow safety steps, and protect yourself and others during a fire emergency.
